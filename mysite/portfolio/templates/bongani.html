About me

My full name is Bongani Cacambile, I have a code B advanced drivers license, a Peace Officer and Traffic Warden Certificate that, I have completed in the Metro Police College in Cape Town Observatory. I have firearm license qualifications in Hand Gun, Hand Gun Business, Shot Gun, Shot Gun Business and also in Military and Law. I have been privileged enough to work and be seconded with a really great Unit like the Metro Police GDTT (Gang and Drug Task Team), Metro Police K-9 Unit, Traffic and S.I.U (Special investigative Unit).

The Stabilisation Unit enforces By-Laws and creates peace and order within a certain area of Jurisdiction and protects Council property especially against Gang violence or Gang infested areas. This is done by conducting stop and searches, house searches and VCP’s (Vehicle Inspections) via “Shot-Spotter” Joint operations with the GDTT, K-9, S.I.U, and Traffic and sometimes accompanied by SAPS (POPS) and OPS Combat.

I hope the above impresses and I look forward to the possible opportunity of an interview with you.
